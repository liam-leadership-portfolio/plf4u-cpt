Self-Analysis

Leadership Style

Relationship-Oriented or Task-Oriented?

As a leader, I am a mix of a relationship-oriented and a task-oriented leader, leaning more towards task-oriented leadership. I believe that you should focus on your tasks first and then start doing what you want. I also believe that to work most efficiently, it is best to know those around you, as it helps a lot to get work done if you have a comfortable environment to work in. I believe in a motto of:

Do what you need to do first before doing what you want to do

I believe that it is best to step up and do what is required to be done before you start doing what isn't required.

True Colours

My true colours are orange and gold. I am usually a just-do-it type of person, where if I feel like doing something, and there is nothing that speaks against it, why not do it?
